Sol Bot Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Sol Bot is a private local assistant setup used by its owner to access their own Google accounts and files.

Data Access

The app may request access to Google account data such as Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Contacts when the account owner grants permission.

Data Use

Data is used only to perform actions requested by the account owner, such as reading email, sending email, managing calendar events, or working with files.

Data Sharing

Data is not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Data Storage

Authentication tokens may be stored locally on the owner's devices or systems for continued access. Google data is not stored for advertising or resale.

Contact

Questions can be sent to solksterling@gmail.com.
